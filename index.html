Squad Effects

Stat Summary:

  • Health: Increases survivability by boosting total hit points.
  • Shield: Adds a regenerating barrier to absorb damage.
  • Armor: Reduces damage taken by mitigating a percentage of incoming hits.
  • Energy: Determines the pool for casting abilities.
  • Sprint Speed: Affects movement speed when sprinting.
  • Duration: Extends the length of ability effects.
  • Efficiency: Reduces the energy cost of abilities.
  • Range: Increases the area of effect for abilities.
  • Strength: Boosts the power of ability effects.
  • Damage: (Weapons) Determines base damage per hit.
  • Fire Rate: (Weapons) Controls how fast the weapon shoots.
  • Crit Chance: (Weapons) Chance to deal a critical hit.
  • Crit Multiplier: (Weapons) Increases damage on critical hits.
